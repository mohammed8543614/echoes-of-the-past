The Process

How It Works

I

Glyph Detection

A YOLO vision model scans the image and identifies each individual hieroglyph, assigning Gardiner classification codes.

II

Direction Analysis

Detected glyphs are sorted into rows and the reading direction — right-to-left or left-to-right — is determined by scoring both orientations.

III

AI Translation

A multilingual transformer model converts the sequence of Gardiner codes into English, row by row, preserving the original structure.
